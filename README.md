# Susquehanna International Group (SIG) (susquehanna-international-group)

Susquehanna International Group (SIG) is a privately held global quantitative trading firm founded in 1987 and headquartered in Bala Cynwyd, Pennsylvania. The firm employs more than 3,500 people across 17+ offices worldwide and operates as one of the largest options market makers globally. SIG's businesses span Quantitative Trading, Predictions, Institutional Sales, Susquehanna Growth Equity (SGE), Sustainable Investments, Asia-focused Venture Capital (notably an early and large stake in ByteDance), and the River's Edge promotional arm. SIG does not operate a public developer portal, expose external trading APIs, or publish open-source repositories under a public GitHub organization — its `sig-com` GitHub org has no public repositories. All technology is internal to the trading and quantitative research platform.
